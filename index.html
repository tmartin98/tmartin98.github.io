About Me

Professional Summary

AI Developer and Data Scientist with 5+ years of hands-on experience building and deploying machine learning systems in production environments. I have delivered end-to-end AI solutions across domains including multilingual document intelligence, computer vision for sports analytics, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines — all within demanding HPC environments using SLURM-managed clusters.

My work sits at the intersection of applied ML research and MLOps: designing robust training pipelines, evaluating and iterating on models, and integrating large language models into client-facing workflows.
